Log 1: Introducing Your Object

For your first log entry, please write 100-200 words, including:

  • a description of the physical composition of the object (including colour, material, and size);
  • an account of where the object came from (both how you acquired it and where, if possible, it was made);
  • the rough cost or value of the object; and
  • why you selected it, including any personal value it has.

In the log entry, feel free to include a photograph of the object, if you wish.

Remember: your log should be named "index.html" and all entries this semester will be saved there.
